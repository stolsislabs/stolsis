# Puzzle & Solo

Create a puzzle or solo game.

::::steps

### Step one

Select the `SOLO` toggle (ignore for puzzle)

### Step two

Click `NEW GAME`

### Step three

Your game will appear at the top of the games list. Click `➡️` to play
